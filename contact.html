FAQs

Consultation and Recovery: Your Questions Answered

How can I schedule a consultation with Dr. Rachana Tataria?

You can easily book a consultation by filling out the contact form on our website or calling our clinic (+91-9036248518) directly.

Where is Dr. Rachana Tataria’s clinic located?

Dr. Tataria practices at Fortis Hospital, Mumbai. Detailed address and directions will be provided upon booking your appointment.

What is breast microvascular reconstruction, and how can it help me?

Breast microvascular reconstruction is an advanced surgical procedure that uses tissue from other parts of your body, such as the abdomen (DIEP flap) or thighs (TUG flap), to rebuild the breast after mastectomy or trauma. Dr. Rachana specializes in these techniques, ensuring natural-looking results and improved confidence.

What body contouring procedures does Dr. Rachana offer?

Dr. Rachana provides personalized body contouring treatments, including liposuction, abdominoplasty, and post-weight-loss sculpting, to enhance body shape and restore confidence. These procedures are tailored to meet individual goals and ensure long-lasting results.

Does Dr. Rachana offer solutions for breast asymmetry?

Yes, Dr. Rachana specializes in correcting breast asymmetry through tailored procedures such as augmentation, reduction, or reconstruction to achieve a balanced and symmetrical appearance.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

Please bring any relevant medical records, imaging reports, and a list of current medications for an informed discussion.

How soon can I resume normal activities after surgery?

Recovery times vary depending on the procedure. Dr. Rachana will provide you with a tailored recovery plan and guidelines to ensure a smooth healing process.

Does Dr. Rachana provide post-surgical care?

Yes, comprehensive post-surgical care is provided to ensure optimal recovery and results. You can reach out to our team via phone or email, and we’ll ensure you’re fully supported during your recovery journey.

What are the costs associated with the procedures?

The cost of surgery depends on the type of procedure and individual patient needs. During your consultation, Dr. Rachana will provide a detailed breakdown of the costs involved.
