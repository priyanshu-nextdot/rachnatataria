Meet Dr. Rachana Tataria

Dr Rachana Tataria is a Consultant Plastic, Reconstructive & Aesthetic Surgeon with special interest in breast reconstruction and aesthetic procedures. Over the years she has trained in top institutions in India & the United Kingdom.

She holds the FRCS Plastic Surgery specialisation from Royal College of Surgeons, UK. In addition, she has completed Royal College of Surgeons accredited Breast Oncoplastic Fellowship from University of Coventry.

She has also completed a Breast Microvascular Surgery Fellowship from University of Manchester and Bristol.

What are Dr. Rachana Tataria’s medical qualifications?

Dr. Rachana holds an MBBS, DNB (General Surgery & Plastic Surgery), MCh (Plastic Surgery), and FRCS (Plastic Surgery, UK).

Where did Dr. Rachana receive her specialized training?

She has trained at top institutions in India and the UK, including Manchester University NHS and University Hospital Coventry & Warwickshire NHS.

What fellowships has Dr. Rachana completed?

She has completed prestigious fellowships in Oncoplastic Breast Surgery and Breast Reconstruction Microsurgery from leading hospitals in the UK.

Has Dr. Rachana contributed to medical research?

Yes, she has published multiple research papers, book chapters, and case studies in plastic, reconstructive, and aesthetic surgery.

What professional organizations is Dr. Rachana affiliated with?

She is a member of the Royal College of Surgeons (UK), Association of Plastic Surgeons of India, and Indian Association of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons.

Does Dr. Rachana participate in medical conferences and training programs?

Yes, she regularly attends and presents at national and international conferences to stay at the forefront of surgical advancements.

What are Dr. Rachana’s key areas of academic focus?

Her academic interests include breast reconstruction techniques, microsurgery, trauma reconstruction, and aesthetic surgery.
