Advanced Plastic & Reconstructive Care for Lasting Confidence
Meet your doctor, a trusted name in plastic surgery at Fortis Hospital, Mumbai, is dedicated to restoring confidence and well-being.
I believe in the art of transformation, not just for your appearance but for your confidence. My mission is to create outcomes that blend precision and compassion, helping you feel whole and empowered. Together, we can celebrate your beauty and embrace the confident life you deserve.
Dr. Rachana Tataria

Globally Acknowledged Surgeon

Dr Rachana Tataria is a Consultant Plastic, Reconstructive & Aesthetic Surgeon with special interest in breast reconstruction and aesthetic procedures. Over the years she has trained in top institutions in India & the United Kingdom.

She holds the FRCS Plastic Surgery specialisation from Royal College of Surgeons, UK. In addition, she has completed Royal College of Surgeons accredited Breast Oncoplastic Fellowship from University of Coventry.

She has also completed a Breast Microvascular Surgery Fellowship from University of Manchester and Bristol.

FAQs

Consultation and Recovery: Your Questions Answered

What is Dr. Rachana Tataria’s specialization?

Dr. Rachana Tataria specializes in plastic, reconstructive, and aesthetic surgery, with expertise in breast reconstruction, body contouring, trauma surgery, and burns.

Where does Dr. Rachana Tataria practice?

She is a Consultant Plastic, Reconstructive & Aesthetic Surgeon at Fortis Hospital, Mumbai, providing advanced surgical care.

What are Dr. Rachana Tataria’s qualifications?

She holds an MBBS, DNB (General Surgery & Plastic Surgery), MCh (Plastic Surgery), and FRCS (Plastic Surgery, UK), along with specialized fellowships in breast reconstruction and oncoplastic surgery.

Does Dr. Rachana have international experience?

Yes, she has trained and worked in leading hospitals in the UK, including Manchester University NHS and University Hospital Coventry & Warwickshire NHS, specializing in breast reconstruction microsurgery.

What surgical techniques does Dr. Rachana use for breast reconstruction?

She is skilled in DIEP flap, TUG flap, LD flap, and local flaps, offering natural and long-lasting breast reconstruction solutions.

Has Dr. Rachana received any awards or recognitions?

Yes, she was awarded “Best Outgoing Postgraduate Student” in Plastic Surgery and has won multiple research awards for her contributions to the field.

Is Dr. Rachana affiliated with any professional organizations?

She is a member of the Royal College of Surgeons (UK), Association of Plastic Surgeons of India, and the Indian Association of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons.
