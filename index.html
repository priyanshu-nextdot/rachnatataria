Meet Dr. Rachana Tataria

 Dr Rachana Tataria is a Consultant Plastic, Reconstructive & Aesthetic Surgeon with special interest in breast reconstruction and aesthetic procedures. Over the years she has trained in top institutions in India & the United Kingdom.

She holds the FRCS Plastic Surgery specialisation from Royal College of Surgeons, UK. In addition, she has completed Royal College of Surgeons accredited Breast Oncoplastic Fellowship from University of Coventry.

She has also completed a Breast Microvascular Surgery Fellowship from University of Manchester and Bristol.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our procedures, care, and results.

What types of surgeries does Dr. Rachana Tataria specialize in?

Dr. Rachana specializes in plastic, reconstructive, and aesthetic surgery, including breast reconstruction, body contouring, trauma surgery, burns, and microsurgery.

Where does Dr. Rachana Tataria practice?

She is a Consultant Plastic, Reconstructive & Aesthetic Surgeon at Fortis Hospital, Mumbai, offering advanced surgical care.

What is the recovery time for plastic and reconstructive surgeries?

Recovery varies based on the procedure, but most patients resume normal activities within a few weeks to a few months, with personalized post-surgery care.

Does Dr. Rachana offer non-surgical aesthetic treatments?

Yes, in addition to surgical procedures, she offers non-surgical aesthetic treatments to enhance natural beauty and restore confidence.

Why choose Dr. Rachana Tataria for plastic and reconstructive surgery?

With over a decade of experience, international training, and expertise in advanced surgical techniques, Dr. Rachana is committed to delivering natural, safe, and long-lasting results.

What body contouring procedures does Dr. Rachana offer?

She offers liposuction, abdominoplasty (tummy tuck), post-weight-loss body sculpting, and other advanced contouring techniques to enhance body shape.
