Breast Augmentation: A Comprehensive Overview

Breast Augmentation is a popular cosmetic surgery designed to enhance the size, shape, and symmetry of the breasts using implants. Whether you're looking to restore volume lost after pregnancy or aging, or simply want to achieve a fuller look, breast augmentation offers a customizable solution. It typically involves the use of saline or silicone implants, tailored to meet the individual needs and aesthetic goals of each patient.

Dr. Rachana Tataria specializes in creating a natural and balanced look that complements your body proportions. The procedure is minimally invasive and is performed with careful consideration of your anatomy, ensuring results that are both beautiful and natural-looking.

Breast augmentation is one of the most sought-after cosmetic surgeries, offering a significant boost in confidence and body image for many women.

What is breast augmentation?

Breast augmentation (Breast enlargement/ ‘Boob job’) is a surgical procedure that involves inserting implants underneath the breast tissue to enhance the size and shape of the breasts.

It’s a popular choice for women looking to improve breast volume, balance or symmetry.

Am I fit to have breast augmentation?

If you are in good health and in good physical condition, ideal body weight and non- smoker, yes you are fit to undergo this procedure to optimise your results.

What are the benefits of breast augmentation?

Breast augmentation can improve body proportions especially if you have relatively small to moderate breast size, enhances your confidence, and provides a youthful appearance.

It can also help restore volume lost after pregnancy, weight fluctuations or aging.

How do I know which type of implant is right for me?

Choosing the right implant depends on factors such as your body type and dimensions, skin quality, desired breast size and projection and personal aesthetic goals.

Dr. Rachana Tataria will help you decide between various types of  implants and guide you in selecting the appropriate shape and size for your body during the consultation.

Are breast implants safe?

 Yes, implants are generally considered safe. Breast implants are made of medical grade silicone which has wide application in the world of medicine. We encourage the patients to educate about the safety of implants before planning the procedure.

Breast implants made of silicone have been subject to guidelines from regulatory bodies and there is enormous amount of data in literature and research to support safety of these implants.

Dr. Rachana Tataria ensures your safety by using the best techniques and providing ongoing care to reduce complications.

Will breast augmentation scars be noticeable?

Scarring from breast augmentation is usually minimal and fades over time. Dr. Tataria uses advanced techniques to place the incisions in discrete locations, such as around the areola, in the lower submammary breast fold, or in the armpit, ensuring minimal visible scarring.

What are the complications associated with breast implants / breast augmentation?

Breast implant augmentation can be associated with early complications like bleeding and haematoma, return to theatre, infection, wound breakdown, seroma, rarely implant extrusion and loss.

Late complications can be capsular contracture which is formation of scar tissue around the implant (mild to severe),implant rupture/rotation, BIA-ALCL which is a rare form of breast implant associated lymphoma and breast implant associated illness, need to change or remove implants.

Please discuss with Dr. Tataria regarding individual risks in detail and to optimize conditions to improve outcomes.

 I do not want implants, is there any other way to improve breast size?

Yes, there are other options to augment breast size. We can use fat grafting or lipofilling during which we harvest fat using liposuction from hips, buttock or thighs and inject into your breasts.

It is suitable for mild to moderate enlargements and may need few stages to get desired volume as part of transferred fat gets absorbed always.

Please discuss regarding this procedure with Dr Tataria for further details.

Will breast augmentation help with breast asymmetry?

Does breast augmentation help restore breast volume after pregnancy?

Yes, many women choose breast augmentation to restore the volume lost during pregnancy, breastfeeding, or weight loss, helping them achieve fuller, firmer breasts.

Is breast augmentation safe for women who have had a mastectomy?

Breast augmentation can be a beneficial option for breast reconstruction following a mastectomy. It helps restore the shape and size of the breast, allowing women to regain a sense of wholeness and confidence.

Procedure of Breast Augmentation

The breast augmentation procedure is typically performed under general anesthesia and takes about 1-2 hours.

During the surgery, Dr. Tataria will make small incisions in discreet locations, where she will insert either saline or silicone implants. The implants are placed either behind the breast tissue or under the chest muscle, depending on your body type and the desired look.
‍
The recovery period is usually brief, with most patients returning to their normal activities within a few weeks. The procedure is safe and involves minimal risk, thanks to Dr. Tataria’s expertise in advanced surgical techniques.

How long does the breast augmentation procedure take?

Breast augmentation typically takes 1-2 hours, depending on the complexity of the surgery. Dr. Tataria uses advanced techniques to ensure a quick and efficient procedure.

What is the recovery time after breast augmentation?

Most patients experience mild discomfort and swelling after surgery, with a recovery time of about 1-2 weeks.

It’s important to follow Dr. Tataria’s aftercare instructions to ensure a smooth recovery and optimal results.

Will I need to stay overnight after surgery?

Breast augmentation is typically an outpatient procedure, so you can go home the same day or sometimes an overnight stay.

However, you may need someone to drive you home after the surgery as the effects of anesthesia wear off.

Are the implants placed under the muscle or over the muscle?

The implants can be placed either under the chest muscle or over it, depending on factors like your body structure and desired look.

Dr. Tataria will discuss the best option for you during your consultation.

Recovery Process of Breast Augmentation

The recovery process for breast augmentation is relatively straightforward. After the surgery, you will be given instructions on how to care for your incisions, manage any discomfort, and what activities to avoid. It is essential to rest during the initial weeks and avoid strenuous activities to ensure proper healing.

You may experience some swelling, bruising, and tightness, which will gradually subside over time. Dr. Tataria will monitor your progress and ensure that you achieve the best possible outcome.

How long does it take to recover from breast augmentation surgery?

Full recovery from breast augmentation takes about 6-8 weeks, but most patients feel well enough to return to work and light activities within 1-2 weeks.

What should I expect during the recovery period?

During recovery, you may experience swelling, bruising, and some discomfort, but these symptoms typically subside after a few days to weeks.

You’ll need to avoid heavy lifting and strenuous activities for about 4-6 weeks.

Will I have to wear a special bra after surgery?

Yes, Dr. Tataria will advise you to wear a surgical bra or compression garment to support the breasts during the healing process. This helps reduce swelling and ensures proper positioning of the implants.

When can I return to exercise after breast augmentation?

Exercise should be avoided for at least 4-6 weeks post-surgery to allow the implants to settle properly.

Dr. Tataria will give you the go-ahead once your body has healed sufficiently.

Most women can breastfeed after breast augmentation, though it depends on the placement of the implants and your individual situation.

Can I have breast screening or mammograms after breast implants?

Following breast augmentation, you can continue with routine self examination and it does not interfere with clinical examination by a doctor, ultrasound, mammograms or MRI.

You may need to have additional views of imaging if the assessment is inadequate.

Are breast implants permanent?

It is difficult to say how long the implants will last. Often you don’t need to change the implants if they are not causing any problems. However, in case of aging changes or complications like capsular contracture, the procedure can be revised.

Regular check-ups with Dr. Tataria can help monitor the condition of your implants.

Choosing the right size, shape, and implant placement is key to achieving a natural appearance. Dr. Tataria will work with you to create a plan that ensures your implants complement your body shape and aesthetic desires.
